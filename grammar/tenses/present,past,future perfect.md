## Core idea

Perfect tenses are used to look back from a point in time at an earlier event, result, experience, or process.

## Which tense to use

| Situation | Tense | Form | Example |
|----------|-------|------|---------|
| связь прошлого с настоящим, результат важен сейчас | Present Perfect | have/has + V3 | I’ve passed my test. |
| действие продолжалось до настоящего момента | Present Perfect Continuous | have/has been + V-ing | I’ve been trying to call you for hours. |
| действие произошло раньше другого момента в прошлом | Past Perfect | had + V3 | I’d already seen the film before we watched it together. |
| процесс длился до определённого момента в прошлом | Past Perfect Continuous | had been + V-ing | I’d been working all day, so I was tired. |
| действие завершится к определённому моменту в будущем | Future Perfect | will have + V3 | I’ll have finished the report by 6 pm. |
| процесс будет длиться до определённого момента в будущем | Future Perfect Continuous | will have been + V-ing | By next month, I’ll have been living here for a year. |

## Main uses

### 1. Measuring time up to a point

| Meaning | Tense | Form | Example |
|--------|-------|------|---------|
| период до настоящего момента | Present Perfect / Present Perfect Continuous | have/has + V3 / have/has been + V-ing | I’ve lived here for five years. / I’ve been living here for five years. |
| период до момента в прошлом | Past Perfect / Past Perfect Continuous | had + V3 / had been + V-ing | By 2020, I’d lived there for five years. / I’d been living there for five years by 2020. |
| период до момента в будущем | Future Perfect / Future Perfect Continuous | will have + V3 / will have been + V-ing | By June, I’ll have lived here for five years. / By June, I’ll have been living here for five years. |

### 2. Direct results of earlier events

| Meaning | Tense | Form | Example |
|--------|-------|------|---------|
| результат виден сейчас | Present Perfect | have/has + V3 | I’ve hurt my back, so I can’t carry the boxes. |
| результат был важен в прошлом | Past Perfect | had + V3 | I’d hurt my back, so I couldn’t carry the boxes. |
| результат будет важен в будущем | Future Perfect | will have + V3 | I’ll have finished the work by noon, so I’ll be free after lunch. |

### 3. Experiences up to a point

| Meaning | Tense | Form | Example |
|--------|-------|------|---------|
| опыт к настоящему моменту | Present Perfect | have/has + V3 | I’ve seen this film three times. |
| опыт к моменту в прошлом | Past Perfect | had + V3 | I’d already seen it ten times by then. |
| опыт к моменту в будущем | Future Perfect | will have + V3 | By next year, I’ll have visited ten countries. |

### 4. Recent news / change / trend

| Meaning | Tense | Form | Example |
|--------|-------|------|---------|
| недавнее событие, важно сейчас | Present Perfect | have/has + V3 | I’ve just seen a squirrel. |
| событие было недавним относительно прошлого момента | Past Perfect | had + V3 | She told me she’d just seen him. |
| событие будет завершено к будущему моменту | Future Perfect | will have + V3 | By tomorrow, they’ll have launched the new version. |

## Simple or Continuous?

| Use | Tense | Form | Example |
|-----|-------|------|---------|
| завершённый результат | Perfect Simple | have/has + V3 / had + V3 / will have + V3 | I’ve passed my test. |
| длительный процесс | Perfect Continuous | have/has been + V-ing / had been + V-ing / will have been + V-ing | I’ve been studying all evening. |
| emphasis on completion | Perfect Simple | have/has + V3 / had + V3 / will have + V3 | I’ve written three emails. |
| emphasis on duration/activity | Perfect Continuous | have/has been + V-ing / had been + V-ing / will have been + V-ing | I’ve been writing emails all morning. |

## State verbs

State verbs usually are not used in continuous forms.

| Meaning | Correct form | Wrong form | Example |
|---------|--------------|------------|---------|
| знать кого-то/что-то в течение времени | have/has known | have/has been knowing | How long have you known them? |
| иметь что-то в течение времени | have/has had | have/has been having | I’ve had this phone for years. |

## Time markers

| Tense | Form | Common markers |
|------|------|----------------|
| Present Perfect | have/has + V3 | already, yet, just, ever, never, for, since |
| Present Perfect Continuous | have/has been + V-ing | for, since, all day, recently, lately |
| Past Perfect | had + V3 | already, before, by the time, when |
| Past Perfect Continuous | had been + V-ing | for, since, before, by the time |
| Future Perfect | will have + V3 | by, by then, by the time, before |
| Future Perfect Continuous | will have been + V-ing | by, for, by the time |

## Common mistakes

| Wrong | Correct | Form | Why |
|------|---------|------|-----|
| I’ve hurt my back last week. | I hurt my back last week. | Past Simple | Present Perfect is not used with finished past time. |
| How long have you been knowing her? | How long have you known her? | Present Perfect | “Know” is usually not used in continuous forms. |

## Quick choice guide

| If you want to say... | Use... | Form |
|-----------------------|---------|------|
| результат важен сейчас | Present Perfect | have/has + V3 |
| процесс длился до сейчас | Present Perfect Continuous | have/has been + V-ing |
| что-то случилось раньше другого прошлого события | Past Perfect | had + V3 |
| процесс длился до прошлого момента | Past Perfect Continuous | had been + V-ing |
| что-то завершится к будущему моменту | Future Perfect | will have + V3 |
| процесс будет длиться до будущего момента | Future Perfect Continuous | will have been + V-ing |
