# 1C - Useful Fixed Phrases

| Word | Part of speech | Russian | Example |
|------------|----------------|---------|---------|
| put it bluntly | fixed phrase | сказать прямо, без смягчения | You spend far too much time on social media. You’re going to lose all your friends soon if you’re not careful, to **put it bluntly**. |
| begin with | fixed phrase | для начала, начнём с того что | I don’t like social media. To **begin with**, everyone’s so negative. |
| cap it all | fixed phrase | в довершение всего | I’ve had a terrible day. And to **cap it all**, I’ve been locked out of my social media account. |
| name but a few | fixed phrase | назвать лишь некоторые, привести лишь несколько примеров | I use loads of social networking sites: Facebook, Twitter and Instagram, to **name but a few**. |
| add insult to injury | fixed phrase | усугубить ситуацию, добавить оскорбление к ущербу | He didn’t reply to my invite. Then, to **add insult to injury**, he planned something for the same day. |
| be perfectly honest | fixed phrase | быть совершенно честным | To **be perfectly honest**, if you post strong opinions on social media, you’ll probably regret it later. |
| cut a long story short | fixed phrase | короче говоря, если коротко | They started the network as a homework project and, to **cut a long story short**, 20 years later it has grown into one of the biggest in the world. |
| make matters worse | fixed phrase | ухудшить положение, сделать ещё хуже | I accidentally upset someone on Twitter today. Then, to **make matters worse**, I sent an angry emoticon by mistake. |
