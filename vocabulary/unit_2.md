# 2A - Trends and Developments

| Word | Part of speech | Russian | Example |
|------|----------------|---------|---------|
| downward trend | N | нисходящий тренд, тенденция к снижению | Due to environmental concerns, there has been a **downward trend** in air travel. |
| tipping point | N | переломный момент, критическая точка | Environmental problems are reaching a **tipping point** from which it won’t be possible to save the planet. |
| reverse the trend | V | обратить тенденцию вспять, изменить тренд | Government campaigns are a good way to **reverse the trend** in unhealthy eating. |
| look set to continue | V | по-видимому, продолжиться; похоже, что продолжится | Electric cars are becoming popular in my country, and this trend **looks set to continue**. |
| pass a milestone | V | достичь важного рубежа | When I hit 20 years, I felt like I’d **passed a milestone** in my career. |
| mark the start of a new era | V | ознаменовать начало новой эры | The development of flexible touchscreens **marks the start of a new era** in mobile devices. |
| on the up | A | на подъёме, растущий, улучшающийся | The use of clean-air technology is definitely **on the up**. |
| revert to | V | возвращаться к чему-либо прежнему | More sports teams are **reverting to** traditional tactics. |
| set the trend | V | задавать тренд, становиться примером для других | The innovative public transport system in my city is **setting the trend** for other cities around the world. |
| look promising | V | выглядеть многообещающе | In general, the future of cancer treatment **looks promising**. |

# 2B - Agreement and Disagreement

| Word | Part of speech | Russian | Example |
|------|----------------|---------|---------|
| get on board with | V | поддержать, согласиться с идеей | Do you think people are likely to **get on board with** the idea of accepting a better job title instead of a pay rise? |
| be willing to | A | быть готовым, согласным | I’d **be willing to** accept the idea if it means I can work from home a couple of days a week. |
| be at odds with | A | быть не согласным с, быть в противоречии с | I can imagine a few others in the office might **be at odds with** the idea though. |
| receive pushback | V | столкнуться с сопротивлением, получить негативную реакцию | If we close the school canteen, we’re likely to **receive some pushback** from both students and teachers alike. |
| come up against resistance | V | столкнуться с сопротивлением | We’ll definitely **come up against some resistance**. |
| be open to | A | быть открытым к, быть готовым рассмотреть | I think most students **are open to** the idea of sharing resources. |
| take issue with | V | возражать против, иметь претензии к | I think most will **take issue with** it. |
| be loath to | A | очень не хотеть, быть крайне не склонным | Personally, I’d **be loath to** share mine. |
| go along with | V | согласиться с, поддержать | I think staff are likely to **go along with** the proposals for flexible working hours. |
| be met with a lukewarm response | V | встретить прохладную реакцию | Given how settled they are now, I think it’s likely to **be met with a lukewarm response**. |


# 2C - Positive and Negative Connotations

| Word | Part of speech | +/- | Russian | Example |
|------|----------------|-----|---------|---------|
| exploit | V | - | эксплуатировать | The company **exploits** staff with few qualifications or experience. |
| employ | V | + | нанимать, трудоустраивать | The company **employs** staff with few qualifications or experience. |
| lively | A | + | оживлённый | My town gets very **lively** on Friday nights. |
| rowdy | A | - | шумный, буйный | My town gets very **rowdy** on Friday nights. |
| charming | A | + | очаровательный | The old town is a **charming** area of the city. |
| run-down | A | - | запущенный, обветшалый | The old town is a **run-down** area of the city. |
| mob | N | - | толпа, агрессивная толпа | A large **mob** of people were outside the council offices. |
| group | N | + | группа | A large **group** of people were outside the council offices. |
| cheap | A | - | дешёвый | Red Farm Park is an area of **cheap** housing. |
| affordable | A | + | доступный по цене | Red Farm Park is an area of **affordable** housing. |
| firm | A | + | твёрдый, решительный | The council is taking **firm** measures to prevent people driving in cycle lanes. |
| harsh | A | - | жёсткий, суровый | The council is taking **harsh** measures to prevent people driving in cycle lanes. |
